# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis (EDA & Business Intelligence)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Superstore dataset to uncover meaningful business insights.

The analysis focuses on understanding:

* Sales performance
* Profitability
* Impact of discounts
* Customer and regional trends

It also includes **SQL-style business analysis** and **dashboard planning**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze sales and profit patterns
* Identify top-performing products and regions
* Understand the impact of discount on profit
* Generate actionable business insights

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Dataset Name:** Superstore Dataset
* **Type:** Structured dataset
* **Description:**
  Contains information about:

  * Orders
  * Customers
  * Products
  * Sales, Profit, Discount

## 📊 EDA Process

### 🔹 1. Descriptive Statistics

* Data shape and structure
* Data types and summary statistics

---

### 🔹 2. Univariate Analysis

* Sales distribution (log-scaled)
* Profit distribution (handling negative values)
* Category and sub-category distribution
* Region and shipping mode analysis

---

### 🔹 3. Bivariate Analysis

* Sales vs Profit
* Discount vs Profit
* Quantity vs Profit

---

### 🔹 4. Multivariate Analysis

* Correlation heatmap
* Relationship analysis between variables

---

## 🧠 Business Questions (SQL-style Analysis)

1. What are the top 10 products by revenue?
2. Which cities generate the highest sales?
3. What is the sales distribution across categories?
4. Which region generates the highest profit?
5. How do discounts impact profit?
6. What is the monthly sales trend?
7. Which products are loss-making

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Sales data is highly skewed with a few high-value transactions
* Profit includes both positive and negative values
* High discounts negatively impact profitability
* Office Supplies dominates sales volume
* Technology category generates higher revenue
* Some products consistently generate losses
* Sales shows seasonal trends over time
